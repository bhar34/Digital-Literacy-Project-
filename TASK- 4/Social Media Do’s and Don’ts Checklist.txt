 Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

 Do’s

1 Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts and comments.
2 Use privacy settings to control who can view your personal information.
3 Share useful and meaningful content related to academics or skills.
4 Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
5 Build a positive digital presence that reflects your personality and goals.

 Don’ts

1 Do not share personal information like phone number, address, or passwords.
2 Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content.
3 Do not engage in cyberbullying or negative arguments online.
4 Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages from unknown sources.
5 Do not overshare private details that could affect your future opportunities.
