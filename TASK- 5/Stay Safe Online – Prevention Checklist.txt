 Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1 Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank.
2 Always verify unknown links, emails, or messages before clicking on them.
3 Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
4 Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
5 Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive transactions.
6 Regularly update your apps and devices to protect against security threats.

 UPI / Financial Safety Tips

1 Remember that entering your UPI PIN is only required to send money, not to receive it.
2 Do not approve unknown payment requests or “collect requests” on UPI apps.
3 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
4 Report any cyber fraud immediately at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
5 Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24×7)
